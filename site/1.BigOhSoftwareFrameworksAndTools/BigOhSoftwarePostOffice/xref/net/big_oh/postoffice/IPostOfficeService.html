33  /**
34   * 
35   * As an uber-thin wrapper around the Apache Commons Email framework, instances
36   * of {@link IPostOfficeService} represent the concept of a "neighborhood" post
37   * office from which an application can send email messages without worrying
38   * about quotidian delivery details. In other words, an
39   * {@link IPostOfficeService} object allows you to send an email message with a
40   * single line of code:
41   * <code>PostOfficeServiceFactory.getInstance().sendMail("foo@bar.com", "Hello ...", "World!");</code>
42   * 
43   * @see PostOfficeServiceFactory
44   * @author davewingate
45   * 
46   */
